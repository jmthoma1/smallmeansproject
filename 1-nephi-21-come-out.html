Inspiration

Inspired by 1 Nephi 21:8–14, “Come Out” is a powerful gospel-soul ballad about God calling His children out of darkness, bondage, and despair into healing, mercy, and light. With soaring vocals, heartfelt emotion, and themes of redemption and restoration, the song celebrates the promise that God sees the forgotten, frees the captive, and lovingly leads His people home.
