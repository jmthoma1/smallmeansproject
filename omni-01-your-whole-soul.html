Inspiration

Inspired by the Book of Omni, “Your Whole Soul” tells the story of five generations of record keepers who faithfully preserved sacred truths through times of faith, pride, war, and change. Each voice adds a brief witness before passing the record to the next, culminating in a heartfelt invitation to come unto Christ. Through every generation, the message remains the same: the Lord remembers His people and asks for more than words or possessions—He asks for your whole soul.
