Inspiration

“Armed With Righteousness” is inspired by 1 Nephi 14 and the vision of the Saints scattered across the earth, “few in number” yet filled with the power of God. Blending cinematic folk-rock with soaring male and female vocals, the song tells of division, endurance, and faith in the face of overwhelming darkness. It is a declaration that true strength is not found in numbers or power, but in the Lamb of God.
