Inspiration

Inspired by Jacob’s powerful sermon in Second Nephi Chapter 9, “O the Greatness” contrasts the cunning of the adversary and the pride, blindness, and foolishness of mankind with the infinite wisdom, mercy, justice, grace, and goodness of God. Through vivid storytelling and triumphant celebration, the song declares Christ’s victory over death and hell and invites all to marvel at the greatness of the Holy One of Israel and His redeeming plan.
