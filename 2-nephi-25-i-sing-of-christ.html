Inspiration

Inspired by 2 Nephi 25, I Sing of Christ is a deeply personal testimony of faith after a lifetime of searching the scriptures, witnessing the rise and fall of kingdoms, and pondering the words of the prophets. In this chapter, Nephi pauses his Isaiah commentary to declare the message behind it all: Jesus Christ. Stripped to a solitary voice and acoustic guitar, the song reflects on grace, covenant promises, and the Savior who remains constant when all else fades, culminating in a simple witness.
