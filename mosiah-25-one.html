Inspiration

Inspired by Mosiah 25, "One People" celebrates the power of listening to one another's stories. As different groups shared their journeys of faith, suffering, deliverance, and hope, strangers became family and hearts were united. This upbeat anthem reminds us that while our paths may be different, compassion, empathy, and a shared faith can transform many voices into one people, moving forward together with joy and purpose.
