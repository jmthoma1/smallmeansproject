Inspiration

Inspired by 1 Nephi 2:9–10, “River and Valley” is an uplifting anthem about spiritual endurance, faith, and steadfast devotion. Using the imagery of a river continually flowing toward righteousness and a valley standing firm and immovable, the song encourages listeners to remain faithful through every storm, trusting God to guide and strengthen them along the journey.
