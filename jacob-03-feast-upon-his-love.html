Inspiration

Jacob 3 is a message of comfort to those who have been wounded by pride, cruelty, and the sins of others. Jacob invites them to lift up their heads, receive the pleasing word of God, and feast upon His love. Like a banquet prepared by a loving Father, God’s mercy, grace, hope, and peace are abundant and freely offered to all. This song celebrates the joy of coming to His table and discovering that His love is greater than we can ever fully receive.
