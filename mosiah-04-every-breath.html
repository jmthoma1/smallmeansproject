Inspiration

In Mosiah 4:19 King Benjamin teaches that everything we have comes from God. The air in our lungs, the strength to rise each day, the love of family, the blessings we enjoy, and the mercy that sustains us are all gifts from His hand. Every Breath is a reflection on gratitude, humility, and our dependence on the Lord. The beggar and the king stand side by side before God, realizing that every breath, every blessing, and every good thing has come from Him.
