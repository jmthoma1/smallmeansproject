Inspiration

Inspired by 1 Nephi 8:10–18 and 35–37, “Come to the Tree” is a heartfelt island-inspired spiritual ballad about seeking the love of God amid the darkness and distractions of the world. With gentle acoustic rhythms, warm vocals, and reflective melodies, the song captures Lehi’s invitation to hold to the iron rod, press through the mist, and partake of the fruit that brings joy, peace, and eternal life.
