Inspiration

Inspired by Mosiah 28, "Every Soul" captures the quiet conviction of the sons of Mosiah as they realize that the hope which changed their own lives was never meant to be kept to themselves. With warm folk harmonies and reflective storytelling, the song celebrates a love that reaches beyond every border, every fear, and every difference, declaring that every life is precious and every soul deserves the chance to know hope.
