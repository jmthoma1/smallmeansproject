Inspired by 2 Nephi 33, Even Though We’ve Never Met imagines Nephi’s final message as a personal letter carried across centuries to future readers. With warmth, humility, and deep charity, he shares the truths and tender mercies that sustained him through life’s trials, hoping they will bless others he will never meet in mortality.
