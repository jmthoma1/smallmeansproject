Inspiration

Inspired by 2 Nephi 28, Wo is a modern warning against spiritual complacency and self-deception. Nephi describes a world that insists “all is well” while slowly drifting from truth. Set against a dark, hypnotic synth-pop backdrop, the song explores the danger of comfortable lies, delayed repentance, and ignoring the voice of God. Each recurring “Wo” sounds like a warning siren, calling listeners to wake up, examine their hearts, and hear the Shepherd’s voice before it is too late.
