Inspiration

Inspired by 1 Nephi 5:8, “Now I Know” is a heartfelt all-female harmony anthem told from Sariah’s perspective as she rejoices in the safe return of her sons from Jerusalem. Blending emotional lead vocals, layered harmonies, and uplifting spiritual energy, the song captures a mother’s journey from fear and uncertainty to gratitude, faith, and a renewed testimony that the Lord protects and guides His children
