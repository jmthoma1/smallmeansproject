Inspiration

Inspired by 1 Nephi 9:6, “The End from the Beginning” is a reflective western gospel ballad about trusting the Lord’s wisdom and guidance even when the road ahead is uncertain. With rich cowboy harmonies, acoustic instrumentation, and frontier-style spiritual warmth, the song testifies that God knows all things from the beginning and faithfully prepares a way for His children through every journey and trial.
