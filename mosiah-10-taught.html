Inspiration

In Mosiah 10:11,17 we learn the Lamanites were taught from childhood to hate the Nephites and believe a distorted version of their history. Generations inherited fear, resentment, and division before they ever knew the people they opposed. This song explores the power of what is passed from one generation to the next-and the life-changing moment when a person learns to recognize God's voice above inherited traditions. Truth, mercy, and faith can break cycles that fear alone cannot.
