Inspiration

Inspired by Mosiah 17, "Truth Doesn't Burn" tells the story of Abinadi's fearless witness before King Noah. Though the prophet was condemned to the flames, his testimony could not be destroyed. One listener-Alma-received the message, and that single act of faith changed generations. The fire consumed the messenger, but not the truth. God's word lives on, lighting hearts and spreading from one faithful witness to another.
