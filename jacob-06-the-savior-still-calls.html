Inspiration

Jacob 6 is a final invitation from a prophet who has spent his life teaching of Christ. After describing the Lord’s tireless labor in the vineyard, Jacob pleads with his people to return with full purpose of heart. The Savior still calls to every wandering soul, offering mercy, hope, and a place in His kingdom. Though the day grows late, His arms remain open. This song celebrates the enduring call of Christ to come home, cleave unto God, and choose life through Him.
