Inspiration

Inspired by Mosiah 26, "As Often" is a joyful Western anthem about second chances and the road home. Like a seasoned cowboy who knows every trail has a way back, it celebrates the promise that no one is beyond mercy. With classic cowboy swing, warm harmonies, and timeless frontier imagery, the song reminds us that whenever we turn our hearts toward God, His welcome is already waiting-today, tomorrow, and as often as we need it.
