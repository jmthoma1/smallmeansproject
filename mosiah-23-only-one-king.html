Inspiration

Inspired by Mosiah 23, "Only One King" celebrates a people who willingly rejected earthly power and chose the Lord as their true King. Blending majestic orchestral grandeur with contemporary pop, this anthem declares that no crown, throne, or kingdom can compare to the peace, hope, and freedom found under His reign. A triumphant song of worship, allegiance, and joyful surrender to the only King who reigns forever.
