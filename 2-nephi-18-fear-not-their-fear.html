Inspiration

Inspired by 2 Nephi 18 (Isaiah 8), Fear Not Their Fear contrasts the noise of a frightened world with the quiet confidence that comes from trusting God. As crowds chase headlines, rumors, and fear, the song calls listeners to stand apart, seek the Lord, and refuse to let panic shape their lives. Blending gospel conviction with modern energy, it reminds us that peace is not found in following the crowd, but in holding fast to God when everyone else is running.
