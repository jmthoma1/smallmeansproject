Inspiration

Inspired by 1 Nephi 1:14, this uplifting arena pop-rock anthem captures Lehi’s awe after seeing heavenly visions and discovering the greatness, mercy, and power of God. The song is a joyful declaration of wonder, praising the Lord whose throne is high above the heavens and whose goodness reaches all the earth.
