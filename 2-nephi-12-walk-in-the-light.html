Inspiration

Inspired by Second Nephi Chapter 12 (Isaiah 2), “Walk in the Light” follows humanity’s journey toward the Mountain of the Lord. As nations leave behind pride, conflict, and division, they are drawn together by God’s light, learning His ways and walking His paths. The song celebrates Isaiah’s vision of peace, unity, and hope—a future where swords become plowshares and all people gather to worship the Lord in harmony and light.
