Inspiration

Inspired by 1 Nephi 4:1–3, “Why Do You Doubt” is a soulful southern gospel anthem about courage, faith, and trusting in the power of God against impossible odds. Drawing on Nephi’s call to remember the miracles of the Red Sea and the deliverance of Israel, the song blends gritty conviction, gospel choir energy, and heartfelt testimony into a bold declaration that the Lord still prepares a way for His people today.
