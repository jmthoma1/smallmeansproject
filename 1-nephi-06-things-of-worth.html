Inspiration

Inspired by 1 Nephi 6:4–6, “Things of Worth” is a warm reggae-inspired spiritual anthem about keeping life centered on what truly matters. With laid-back island rhythms, soulful melodies, and reflective lyrics, the song encourages listeners to let go of worldly distractions and seek the things that bring lasting peace, faith, truth, and salvation through God.
