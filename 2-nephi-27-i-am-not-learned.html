Inspiration

Inspired by 2 Nephi 27, I Am Not Learned celebrates the surprising way God accomplishes His work through humble, ordinary people. Drawing on the contrast between the learned who could not read the sealed book and the meek who rejoice in the Lord, the song reminds listeners that faith often sees what pride cannot. With themes of revelation, humility, and divine purpose, it testifies that God still speaks, still guides, and still works through willing hearts to accomplish extraordinary things.
