Inspiration

Inspired by 1 Nephi 20:10, “Refined in the Furnace” is a cinematic rock anthem about enduring trials, surviving affliction, and being transformed by God through the fire. Thunderous drums, explosive choruses, and powerful vocals capture the journey from fear and brokenness to strength and faith. A bold spiritual anthem about becoming stronger through the flames instead of being consumed by them.
