Inspiration

From Mosiah 7, King Limhi's people are trapped in bondage and searching for hope. As Ammon recounts what happened to Zeniff's colony, the story opens the door to some of the Book of Mormon's most dramatic accounts still to come-pride, repentance, prophets, kings, captivity, deliverance, and unexpected heroes. This song playfully teases the adventures ahead, inviting listeners to lean in and keep reading. The best parts of the story are still waiting just around the corner.
