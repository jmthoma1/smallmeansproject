Inspiration

Inspired by Mosiah 20, "There's More to the Story" reminds us that appearances can be deceiving and quick judgments often miss the truth. As Gideon urged King Limhi to remember the words of Abinadi before going to war, the story turns from anger to understanding. Through rich vocal harmonies, this song celebrates the wisdom of listening, seeking truth, and remembering that there is always more to the story.
