Inspiration

Inspired by Mosiah 21, "When We Finally Knelt" tells the story of a people who exhausted every earthly solution before humbling themselves before God. Though deliverance did not come immediately, the Lord heard their prayers, lightened their burdens, and quietly prepared a way forward. This uplifting duet celebrates the truth that surrender is not defeat-it is often the first step toward hope, healing, and divine deliverance.
