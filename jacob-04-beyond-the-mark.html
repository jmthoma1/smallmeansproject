Inspiration

Jacob 4 teaches that spiritual blindness often comes not from a lack of truth, but from looking past the simple truths God has already revealed. In seeking mysteries, pride, or hidden knowledge, people can miss the Savior standing plainly before them. This song reflects on the danger of looking beyond the mark and the peace that comes from returning to Christ, embracing simple faith, and trusting the clear and precious truths He has given.
