Inspiration

Inspired by Mosiah 16:7-15, "Death Is Swallowed Up" is a triumphant declaration that Jesus Christ has conquered the grave. Abinadi proclaims that through the Redeemer, the bands of death are broken, the sting of death is swallowed up, and resurrection is promised to all. From quiet uncertainty to victorious celebration, this rock opera rejoices in the hope, power, and eternal life found through the Holy One of Israel.
