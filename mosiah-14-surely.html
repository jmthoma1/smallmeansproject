Inspiration

Inspired by Mosiah 14, "Surely" is Isaiah's confession that we misunderstood the Savior. We saw His suffering and assumed He was being punished, never realizing He was carrying our griefs, our sorrows, and our sins. The burden was His to bear, but it should have been ours. In quiet harmony, this song celebrates the redeeming love of Jesus Christ, who willingly took our place so we could come home.
