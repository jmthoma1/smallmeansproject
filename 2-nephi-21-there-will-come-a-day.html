Inspiration

Inspired by 2 Nephi 21 (Isaiah 11), There Will Come a Day is a hopeful duet that looks beyond the struggles of the present to the peace of Christ’s millennial reign. Through images of wolves and lambs dwelling together, nations at rest, and a world healed from sorrow, the song celebrates God’s promise to renew the earth. Blending longing with assurance, it testifies that the Prince of Peace will one day make all things right and fill the earth with His glory.
