Inspiration

Jacob 1 marks the beginning of a new generation. With Nephi gone, Jacob accepts the sacred responsibility of teaching, warning, and testifying of Christ. He reminds us that every disciple has a calling to magnify—whether through leadership, service, compassion, or quiet faithfulness. This song celebrates the courage to rise, use our gifts, lift others, and let the Savior’s light shine through our lives as we build His kingdom one soul at a time.
