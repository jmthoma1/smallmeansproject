Inspiration

From Mosiah 8:17 King Limhi rejoices when he learns that God provides seers-prophets who can reveal hidden things, uncover truth, and bring lost knowledge into the light. This song celebrates the divine gift of revelation and the men and women God calls to help others see what they could never discover alone. Whether hidden records, forgotten truths, or personal answers, the Lord still guides His children by bringing darkness into light and turning uncertainty into understanding.
