Inspiration

Inspired by Mosiah 22, "Home" celebrates the joyful deliverance of Limhi's people as the Lord quietly fulfills the escape He had been preparing all along. While they thought they were trapped, Heaven was already opening doors, softening hearts, and making a way. With infectious energy and gratitude, this song reminds us that God's greatest miracles often begin long before we recognize them-and every step of faith leads us closer to home.
