Inspiration

Inspired by the prophecy in 2 Nephi 3:13 that the Lord can make weak things become strong. This song tells the story of feeling unprepared, inadequate, and afraid when Heaven calls — yet discovering that God does not wait for perfection before He sends His children forward. Through faith, grace, and divine strength, weakness becomes courage and ordinary people become instruments in the Lord’s hands.
