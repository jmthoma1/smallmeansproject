Inspiration

Inspired by the servant imagery in Second Nephi Chapter 7 (Isaiah 50), “No Shame” is a soulful conversation between a weary believer and the Savior. As fear, opposition, and weakness press in, the Lord gently reminds His child: “My back was given for you.” Through Christ’s suffering and sacrifice, shame loses its power and faith becomes strength. An uplifting blues anthem about redemption, resilience, and standing unashamed with the Lord beside us.
