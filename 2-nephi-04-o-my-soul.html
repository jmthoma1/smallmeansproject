Inspiration

Inspired by Nephi’s psalm in 2 Nephi 4, this song captures the deeply human struggle between faith and weakness, sorrow and hope. After reflecting on his fears, failures, and spiritual battles, Nephi ultimately turns back to God with praise and trust. “O My Soul” is a cinematic prayer about remembering the Lord’s mercy, fighting through darkness, and finding strength to rejoice again in the rock of our salvation.
