Inspiration

Inspired by 2 Nephi 32, The Conversation explores the simple but profound truth that prayer is only the beginning of communication with God. Through scripture, the Holy Ghost, trusted friends, quiet impressions, and moments of stillness, the Lord continues to speak to His children. Written as a warm, reflective singer-songwriter ballad, the song follows a traveler who spent years doing all the talking before discovering that heaven was never silent. The conversation begins when we learn to listen.
