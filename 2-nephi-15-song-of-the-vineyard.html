Inspiration

Inspired by 2 Nephi 15 (Isaiah 5), The Song of the Vineyard tells the Lord’s tender lament over a vineyard He lovingly planted and cared for. He cleared the stones, built the tower, prepared the winepress, and gave every blessing needed for growth—yet the vineyard brought forth wild grapes. Through imagery of vines, harvest, and a watchful keeper, this song explores divine love, agency, sorrow, and the enduring invitation to return home before the final harvest.
