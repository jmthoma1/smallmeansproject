Inspiration

Inspired by Words of Mormon, this song explores the peace that comes from trusting God without having all the answers. Life is filled with questions, unexpected turns, and unseen purposes, but faith grows when we learn to trust the One who sees the end from the beginning. Rather than demanding certainty, the song celebrates confidence in divine guidance, reminding us that we don’t need to know everything when God lovingly leads us one step at a time.
