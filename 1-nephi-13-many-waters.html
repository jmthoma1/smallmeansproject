Waited somewhere past the stars

He could never quite explain

Like a voice beyond the waters

Calling softly through the rain

And the Spirit of God

Wrought upon the man

Through the restless sea

And the shifting sands

Past the fear of men

Past the great unknown

There was something in the west

Calling him home

Every night the crew grew restless

Certain death was waiting there

Wrought upon the man

Inspiration

“Many Waters” is inspired by 1 Nephi 13:12 and the vision of a man carried across the ocean by the Spirit of God. Blending cinematic folk-rock with the rhythm of the sea, the song follows a restless soul drawn westward toward an unseen shore. Through storm, doubt, and endless waves, it tells the story of divine purpose moving across the waters to prepare the way for something greater.
