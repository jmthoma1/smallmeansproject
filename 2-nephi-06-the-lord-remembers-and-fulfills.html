Inspiration

Inspired by Jacob’s teachings in Second Nephi Chapter 6, this anthem celebrates the Lord’s covenant promise to gather, restore, and redeem His people. Though generations pass and nations rise and fall, God does not forget those who trust in Him. “The Lord Remembers and Fulfills” is a triumphant declaration of hope, prophecy, and the certainty that every promise spoken by the Holy One of Israel will one day be fulfilled.
