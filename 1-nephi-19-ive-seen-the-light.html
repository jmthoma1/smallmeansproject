Inspiration

Inspired by 1 Nephi 19:18, “I’ve Seen the Light” is an upbeat swing-jazz testimony about recognizing God’s hand through deserts, storms, and impossible journeys. Filled with joyful brass, rhythmic piano, and hopeful energy, the song celebrates Christ’s redeeming love and the desire to share that light with the world. A spirited anthem of faith, gratitude, and witness.
