Inspiration

In a world full of competing voices, opinions, and promises, King Benjamin teaches a simple truth: God asks one thing-keep His commandments. Unlike the world, the Lord never changes, never breaks His word, and never varies from what He has promised. One Thing is an energetic rock anthem about trusting God's steady voice, following His path, and discovering that His promises are as dependable today as they have always been.
