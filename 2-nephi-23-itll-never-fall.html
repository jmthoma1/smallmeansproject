Inspiration

Inspired by 2 Nephi 23 (Isaiah 13), It’ll Never Fall is a humorous barbershop quartet satire about the pride of Babylon and the foolish belief that human achievements can endure forever. Through witty harmonies and exaggerated boasts, the song follows a city convinced of its own greatness—until history proves otherwise. Beneath the laughter lies Isaiah’s warning: every kingdom built on wealth, power, and self-glory eventually crumbles, but the kingdom of God alone stands forever.
