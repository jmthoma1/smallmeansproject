Inspiration

Inspired by 2 Nephi 29, Bigger Than You Think celebrates a God whose love, voice, and remembrance extend far beyond any one nation, language, or people. Blending musical influences from around the world, the song answers Nephi’s question: “Do ye not suppose that there are more nations than one?” It is a joyful anthem of continuing revelation, global unity, and divine belonging, proclaiming that heaven is not silent, God still speaks, and His family is far bigger than we imagine.
