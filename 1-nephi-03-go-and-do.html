Inspiration

Inspired by 1 Nephi 3:7, “I Will Go and Do” is an uplifting spiritual anthem about courage, obedience, and trusting God even when the path ahead is uncertain. Built around Nephi’s declaration of faith, the song celebrates the promise that the Lord prepares a way for His children to accomplish all He commands, combining determination, hope, and unwavering trust in divine guidance.
