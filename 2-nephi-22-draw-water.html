Inspiration

Inspired by 2 Nephi 22 (Isaiah 12), Draw Water is a joyful African choral celebration of God’s saving power. Centered on Isaiah’s invitation to “draw water out of the wells of salvation,” the song traces the life-giving water that flows throughout scripture—from Hagar’s well and Moses’ rock to Jordan’s waters and Christ, the Living Water. Through vibrant call-and-response singing and communal praise, it rejoices in the God who quenches every thirst and fills every soul with hope, life, and salvation
