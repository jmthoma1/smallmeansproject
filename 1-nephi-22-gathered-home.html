Inspiration

Inspired by 1 Nephi 22:25, “Gathered Home” is an uplifting British Invasion-style harmony song about hope, redemption, and God gathering His children out of darkness and distance into light and belonging. With jangling guitars, layered harmonies, and joyful singalong energy, the song celebrates the promise that no soul is forgotten and every faithful journey ultimately leads home.
