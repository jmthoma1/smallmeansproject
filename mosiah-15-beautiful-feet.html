Inspiration

Inspired by Mosiah 15:15-18, "Beautiful Feet" celebrates the joyful message of Jesus Christ echoing from mountain to mountain. Abinadi teaches that those who proclaim peace and bring good tidings are blessed, carrying hope to every valley and every heart. Like voices calling across the alpine peaks, the gospel invites all to rejoice in the Redeemer and share the good news that salvation has come through Him.
