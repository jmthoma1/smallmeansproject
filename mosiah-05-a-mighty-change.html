Inspiration

In Mosiah 5:2 after hearing King Benjamin's powerful testimony of Jesus Christ, the people declare that they have experienced a mighty change in their hearts. No longer desiring evil, they are filled with faith, joy, and a willingness to follow God. This song celebrates the transforming power of Christ-a change deeper than behavior, reaching the very heart. Through His grace, ordinary people become new creatures, choosing goodness, covenant discipleship, and lasting spiritual peace.
