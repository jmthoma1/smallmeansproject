Inspiration

Jacob 2 teaches that wealth is not an end in itself but a blessing that can be used to lift others. Jacob invites us to seek the kingdom of God first, then use whatever abundance comes our way to feed the hungry, clothe the needy, comfort the afflicted, and bring relief to those who suffer. This song celebrates generosity, compassion, and the joy that comes from using God’s gifts for the intent to do good.
