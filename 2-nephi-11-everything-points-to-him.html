Inspiration

Inspired by 2 Nephi 11, this song celebrates Nephi’s testimony that all things given by God point to Jesus Christ. From ancient sacrifices and temple worship to prophets, scriptures, stars, and promises, every symbol and story testifies of Him. With joyful conviction, the song explores how Christ is woven through God’s plan from the beginning of the world. Every sign, every song, every act of faith ultimately leads to the Savior—everything points to Him.
