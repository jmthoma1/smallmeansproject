Inspiration

Inspired by 1 Nephi 7:4-6, 19; 16:7; 17:1-3, “Beyond Jerusalem” is an uplifting harmony-driven anthem told from the perspective of the daughters of Ishmael as they leave their home and journey into the unknown wilderness by faith. Blending emotional female harmonies, hopeful melodies, and themes of courage, sacrifice, and trust in God’s promises, the song celebrates the strength it takes to move forward when Heaven calls beyond what is familiar.
