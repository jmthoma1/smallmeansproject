Inspiration

Inspired by 2 Nephi 24 (Isaiah 14), I Will Ascend explores the seductive power of pride through the voice of one who seeks to exalt himself above all others. As ambition grows into self-worship, a prophetic voice interrupts the illusion with eternal truth: every throne built on pride must fall. Set against the imagery of Babylon and the fallen morning star, the song contrasts human arrogance with God’s sovereignty, reminding listeners that the Lord alone is high.
