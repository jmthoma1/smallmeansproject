Inspiration

Inspired by Mosiah 27, "Someone Was Praying" tells the story of Alma the Younger through the unseen miracle that changed everything-not the angel's appearance, but the faithful prayers that came long before it. Told as an energetic narrative, the song reminds us that heaven hears every sincere prayer, and that someone else's faith may be preparing a miracle we cannot yet see. Sometimes the greatest answers begin while someone is still praying.
