Inspiration

“Unexpected” is inspired by 1 Nephi 16 and the journey of a family learning that faith is not found in perfect plans, but in trusting God through the turns they never saw coming. With warm folk melodies and reflective storytelling, the song explores loss, guidance, uncertainty, and grace—showing how even the unexpected moments of life can shape us into who we are meant to become.
