Inspiration

Inspired by 1 Nephi 10:17, “I Want to Know for Myself” is a gritty folk-gospel anthem about seeking personal revelation and discovering truth through faith. Fueled by raw acoustic energy, soulful vocals, and uplifting harmonies, the song captures Nephi’s desire to move beyond inherited testimony and experience the power of God firsthand, reminding listeners that all who diligently seek Him can come to know the truth for themselves.
