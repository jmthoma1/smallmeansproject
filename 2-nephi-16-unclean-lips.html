Inspiration

Inspired by 2 Nephi 16 (Isaiah 6), Unclean Lips tells Isaiah’s deeply personal encounter with the Lord. Surrounded by glory, fire, and heavenly voices, he suddenly sees himself as he truly is—flawed, exposed, and unworthy. Yet instead of condemnation, he receives mercy. A coal from the altar purges his sin, grace replaces fear, and the man who entered the temple leaves forever changed. This song is a reflection on humility, redemption, and the transforming power of God’s presence.
