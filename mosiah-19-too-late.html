Inspiration

Inspired by Mosiah 19, "Too Late" is told through the eyes of one of King Noah's soldiers who witnessed Abinadi's trial, the king's downfall, and the fulfillment of the prophet's words. Looking back with deep regret, he realizes that truth never changed-only the hearts that rejected it. The song is a reminder that prophets speak in mercy before judgment comes, inviting us to listen today rather than discover the truth when it is too late.
