Inspiration

Inspired by Isaiah’s memorable question in 2 Nephi 20: “Shall the axe boast itself against him that heweth therewith?”, It Wasn’t the Axe is a humorous Irish folk pub song that pokes fun at pride and self-importance. Through a cast of boastful tools—a swaggering axe, a bragging saw, a proud rod, and a self-important staff—the song reminds us that no tool is greater than the hand that guides it. Beneath the laughter lies a timeless truth: every gift, victory, and blessing comes from the Master
