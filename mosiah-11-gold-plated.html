Inspiration

King Noah's kingdom looked prosperous, powerful, and successful. Grand buildings rose, celebrations continued, and everything appeared to shine. But beneath the gold and glamour, the people were drifting farther from God. Inspired by Mosiah 11, this upbeat rock-and-roll anthem explores the danger of valuing appearance over substance and comfort over truth. Some things can be covered with gold, but the condition of a heart can never be hidden from the Lord.
